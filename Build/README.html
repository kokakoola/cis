TFS is good for some things, I would add Asana for tracking and Slack for communicationg and Usersnap[https://usersnap.com/] for onscreen commenting. Fonts and directions

We go with the bulletproofed Google fonts: Roboto (light, Normal, Medium e.g. 300, 400, 500) for english version, Noto for arabic and kurdish. Thats for the webpage. All the documents influenced by MS Word and possibly sent by email shall use unicode fonts.

Other suggestionst for arabic webfonts by community, in Google fonts are arabic-persian fonts under early access fonts. Note that arabic and kurdish letters are not the same.

Pay attention that rtl-writing starts from end - in our understanding. They take the file and start to fill it from end.

As we are going with scss version, there is a helper for rtl-support
